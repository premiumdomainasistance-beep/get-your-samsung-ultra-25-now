The Story

Dive into a world where love and darkness collide

Elena Volkov has spent years building walls around her heart, determined never to let anyone close enough to hurt her again. But when she crosses paths with Adrian Blackwood, those walls begin to crumble.

Adrian is everything she should avoid—a man with a dangerous reputation and secrets that could destroy them both. Yet the attraction between them is undeniable, a magnetic pull that neither can resist.

As their worlds collide, Elena discovers that Adrian's darkness mirrors her own. Together, they navigate a relationship built on passion, trust issues, and the ghosts of their pasts. But when outside forces threaten to tear them apart, they must decide if their love is strong enough to survive the venom that surrounds them.

Content Warning: This book contains mature themes, explicit content, and situations that some readers may find disturbing. Recommended for readers 18+.

Main Characters

Meet the complex souls at the heart of this dark romance
